## Best practices

Engine reconstructs signers for your backend wallets to sign and send transactions. Follow best practices to secure access to your wallets and data.

- Securely store **access tokens** and **thirdweb secret keys**.
- Use **labels** to keep track of your wallets, admins, access tokens.
- Use access token expirations to grant time-bound access. 
- Regularly review the **admins** list to remove inactive and former team members.

## Data privacy

- Engine stores configuration in the provided encrypted in transit and at rest.
- Backend wallet signers are reconstructed only in memory on your Engine instance, and this data is never sent to thirdweb or other external platforms.
