## Get a Metamask Wallet

First of all, we need to have a wallet, we are gonna use Metamask, if you don't have one, you can follow [this guide](/guides/create-a-metamask-wallet).

## Add Polygon Mumbai Testnet to your Network list

We'll go to [chainlist.org](https://chainlist.org/), search "mumbai", and click **"Connect Wallet"**.

![](/assets/portal/guides/get-matic-on-polygon-mumbai-testnet-faucet/get-matic-1.png)

Then, our wallet will be connected and the button will change, click **"Add to Metamask"**.

![](/assets/portal/guides/get-matic-on-polygon-mumbai-testnet-faucet/get-matic-2.png)

This will open a confirmation on our Metamask browser extension, click **"Switch Network"**.

![](/assets/portal/guides/get-matic-on-polygon-mumbai-testnet-faucet/get-matic-3.png)

Great! You should see something like this when you open your Metamask. Click **"Copy to Clipboard"** on the address.

![](/assets/portal/guides/get-matic-on-polygon-mumbai-testnet-faucet/get-matic-4.png)

## Get MATIC from the official faucet

So let's get some MATIC, go to [https://faucet.polygon.technology/](https://faucet.polygon.technology/) which is the official Polygon Mumbai Testnet Faucet.

Paste the address you just copied and click **"Submit"**.

![](/assets/portal/guides/get-matic-on-polygon-mumbai-testnet-faucet/get-matic-5.png)

Click "Confirm" on the dialog that opens.

![](/assets/portal/guides/get-matic-on-polygon-mumbai-testnet-faucet/get-matic-6.png)

You should see something like this!

![](/assets/portal/guides/get-matic-on-polygon-mumbai-testnet-faucet/get-matic-7.png)

After a couple minutes, check your Metamask again, you'll see that you will have received 1 MATIC!

![](/assets/portal/guides/get-matic-on-polygon-mumbai-testnet-faucet/get-matic-8.png)
